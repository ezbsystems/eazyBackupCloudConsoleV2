Controlled Goods Program

Canadian program for sensitive goods and data environments

✓

The Controlled Goods Program regulates how certain sensitive goods and related data are handled in Canada. As a registered organization, our program includes:

  • Screened personnel only – Employees in controlled roles complete formal personnel screening, including criminal record checks, fingerprinting, and security interviews, before being granted access.
  • Physical and logical access controls – Role-based access, logging, and auditability across both facilities and systems.
  • Defined onboarding and offboarding – Documented processes for granting, reviewing, and revoking access for authorized users.

Multi-User Storage That Mirrors How You Work

Every customer, team, or application gets its own user, buckets, and API keys. You keep one bill and one control plane, while e3 keeps data, credentials, and permissions cleanly separated in the background.

Use primary users for customers or departments, then attach subusers for backup agents, services, and integrations. You decide who can see buckets, who can rotate keys, and who is locked to read-only access. When someone leaves or a project ends, you just revoke their user — no key hunting, no shared logins.
